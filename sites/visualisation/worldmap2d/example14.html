Example 14: Show multiple datasources

Map can show data from multiple sources. You also can add and remove data on your needs. It's just done like adding and removing data at any other component.

Attention

This functionallity is subject to change. Currently it is not realised inside the component. In future this function will be moved to be integrated functionality of the Worldmap2d.
