Example 2: Control playback timing for slides

You can set duration, autostart and autostop presentation for slides.
You can set the maximum playback time of any slide useing the attribute "duration". Press space to start the presentation at the first image. Slide changes automatically after 5 seconds. After the default slide time presentation stops after the second image.
Switch to the water slide to see the autostart and autostop effect. This starts playing the presentation when entering the slide and automatically stopping it when the video is at its end.
Manually you can switch with the arrow keys to the next or prev slide.
