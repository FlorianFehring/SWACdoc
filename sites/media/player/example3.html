Example 3: Playback audio effects

You can set a volume for each slide and apply playback effects like fade in or fade out.
Switch to music slide to hear fadein and fadeout effects.
Manually you can use the downarrow key to fadeout a playback.
Attribute "volume" controls volume for that slide relative to the systems volume.
Attributes "fadein" and "fadeout" change the volume from and to zero in the seconds given in this attribute.
